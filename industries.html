Industries We Serve

Every industry has its own risks and its own way of operating. We build security programs around the realities of your business, not around a generic template.

Security Built for Your Industry

We have spent years learning the security challenges that different industries face in New York City. That experience shows up in how we staff, train, and manage every assignment.

Retail

Retail and Shopping Centers

Retail environments deal with shoplifting, organized retail crime, employee safety concerns, and customer disputes. Our retail security officers are trained to spot suspicious behavior early, de-escalate situations, and maintain a presence that keeps both customers and staff comfortable.

Loss prevention and shoplifting deterrence
Customer and employee safety
Parking lot and common area patrols
Holiday and high-traffic surge coverage

Logistics

Warehouses and Distribution Centers

Warehouses and distribution facilities are prime targets for theft, and the losses can add up fast. Our guards monitor dock activity, verify shipments, control access to storage areas, and patrol truck courts to keep unauthorized individuals out.

Dock and loading area monitoring
Shipment and seal verification
Truck court and perimeter patrols
Organized theft prevention protocols

Construction

Construction Sites

Construction sites in New York face persistent security problems. Expensive tools and materials, copper wiring, and heavy equipment are all attractive targets. We assign guards who understand how construction sites operate and adjust coverage as the project moves through phases.

Gate access and credential verification
Tool and copper theft prevention
Laydown and staging area patrols
Phase-adjusted coverage plans
