Armed Security

Protection for High-Risk Environments

Certain locations demand more than a visible security presence. Banks, jewelry retailers, pharmaceutical distributors, and critical infrastructure facilities all face elevated threat levels that require officers trained and licensed to carry a firearm while on duty.

Blue Core Shield provides New York State licensed armed security guards who have completed rigorous weapons qualification, scenario-based training, and ongoing recertification. Every officer assigned to an armed post has been thoroughly vetted and understands the legal responsibilities that come with carrying a weapon on the job.

Our armed security programs are not one-size-fits-all. We work with each client to develop post orders, response protocols, and escalation procedures that match the specific risks at your location. Whether you need a single officer at a retail storefront or a multi-person team at a distribution center, we build the coverage around your operations.

Who This Is For

Ideal Clients for Armed Security

Armed security is best suited for locations that handle high-value assets, face elevated threat levels, or operate in areas with above-average risk.

Banks and Financial Institutions

Branch locations, ATM facilities, and cash handling operations that require a visible armed deterrent.

Jewelry and Luxury Retail

High-value retail environments where inventory theft poses a significant financial risk.

Warehouses and Distribution Centers

Facilities storing valuable goods, electronics, or pharmaceuticals that need protected access points.

Critical Infrastructure

Utilities, data centers, and government-adjacent facilities with strict security requirements.

Corporate Offices

Executive suites and corporate headquarters where personal safety concerns warrant armed protection.

High-Value Transport

Escort and on-site protection during the movement of valuable assets between locations.
