Construction Security

Keeping Job Sites Secure at Every Phase

Active construction sites are open targets. Expensive tools, raw materials, copper wiring, heavy equipment, and building supplies are all vulnerable to theft, especially during nights and weekends when the crew is off site. Add in the risk of unauthorized entry, vandalism, and liability from trespassers getting injured, and the case for dedicated security becomes clear.

Our construction security guards are specifically trained for the unique challenges of job site protection. They manage gate access, verify worker credentials, log deliveries, and conduct regular patrols of the site perimeter. During off-hours, they serve as the sole line of defense between your investment and the people looking to take advantage of an empty site.

We work closely with general contractors and project managers to understand each site's layout, schedule, and specific vulnerabilities. As the project progresses and the site changes, we adjust our security plan accordingly. From foundation work through final inspections, your site stays protected.
