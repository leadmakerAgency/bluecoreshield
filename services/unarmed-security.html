Unarmed Security

The Foundation of Property Protection

For the majority of commercial and residential properties, a trained unarmed security officer is the right fit. These officers provide a professional presence that deters criminal activity while remaining approachable to tenants, employees, and visitors. They are the people who greet guests, manage access points, and handle day-to-day security tasks that keep a building running smoothly.

Our unarmed guards are trained in access control, visitor management, patrol procedures, conflict de-escalation, and emergency response. They follow detailed post orders that we develop in collaboration with each client, so there is never any ambiguity about what is expected on shift.

Whether you need a lobby officer for a residential tower, a roving guard for a shopping center, or overnight coverage for an office building, our unarmed security program delivers consistent, reliable service. We handle scheduling, supervision, and quality checks so you can focus on running your property.
