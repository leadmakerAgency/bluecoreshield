Mobile Patrol

Broad Coverage Without a Standing Post

Not every property needs a full-time guard stationed at one location. Mobile patrols offer an effective alternative for large campuses, multi-building complexes, parking structures, and properties where the security need is more about perimeter checks and visible deterrence than constant on-site presence.

Our patrol officers drive marked vehicles on randomized routes, making their presence felt without following a predictable pattern. Each patrol includes perimeter walks, access point checks, and a visual inspection of the property. If something looks off, the officer documents it immediately and follows established response protocols.

Every patrol vehicle is equipped with GPS tracking, so you always have a verifiable record of when your property was checked. We provide detailed reports after each visit, and our dispatch team is available around the clock to coordinate with patrol officers in real time.
