Our Security Services

From standing posts to mobile patrols, every service we offer is built around clear protocols, trained personnel, and consistent execution.

What We Offer

Security Programs for Every Situation

We do not believe in one-size-fits-all security. Each program is tailored to the specific risks, layout, and operational needs of your property.

Static Coverage

Armed Security Guards

Some locations carry higher risk levels that call for an armed security presence. Banks, jewelry retailers, high-value warehouses, and critical infrastructure facilities all benefit from officers who are trained and licensed to carry a firearm on post.

Our armed guards go through extensive weapons qualification, use-of-force training, and scenario-based drills before they are assigned to any client site.

Officers trained to meet state licensing requirements
Regular firearms qualification and recertification
Use-of-force and de-escalation training
Suitable for banks, jewelry, and critical infrastructure

Mobile Patrol Services

Mobile patrols are an effective way to cover large areas, multiple buildings, or properties that do not need a full-time standing post. Our marked patrol vehicles run randomized routes that keep the coverage pattern unpredictable.

Fire Watch Services

When fire alarm or sprinkler systems are out of service, New York City fire code requires a trained fire watch guard on site. We deploy certified fire watch personnel who understand the regulations and take the job seriously.

FDNY-compliant documentation and logs
Continuous patrol rounds on strict intervals
Trained for evacuation and emergency response
Available for short-notice deployments

Construction Site Security

Active construction sites face constant security challenges. Tool theft, material loss, copper wire stripping, unauthorized access, and vandalism are all common problems. Our construction site guards are specifically trained to manage these risks.
