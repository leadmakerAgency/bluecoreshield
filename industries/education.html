Education Security

Security for Schools and Campuses

Schools and universities need a security approach that puts student safety first while maintaining an atmosphere where learning can happen. Students, parents, and faculty all need to feel that the campus is a safe place, and that means security officers who know how to be present without being overbearing.

Blue Core Shield provides campus security for K-12 schools, colleges, vocational training centers, and private academies across New York City. Our officers are trained to work with students and staff in a way that feels supportive rather than intimidating. They understand that every interaction on a school campus carries weight, and they act accordingly.

We handle visitor screening, perimeter patrols, after-school coverage, event security, and emergency preparedness coordination. Our officers work closely with school administrators to ensure that security protocols align with the school's culture and policies.

What We Cover

Typical Coverage Positions

School security requires officers who are patient, observant, and able to connect with students and staff in a positive way.

Main Entrance and Visitor Check-In

Officers who screen every visitor entering the building, verify identification, and ensure that only authorized individuals gain access to the campus.

Campus and Building Patrols

Regular walkthroughs of hallways, stairwells, restrooms, and outdoor areas to maintain a visible presence and catch problems early.

Arrival and Dismissal Coverage

Officers stationed at drop-off and pick-up zones during morning arrival and afternoon dismissal to manage traffic flow and student safety.

Athletic and Event Posts

Security staffing for sporting events, school dances, performances, and graduation ceremonies where crowd management is needed.
