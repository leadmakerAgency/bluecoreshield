Construction Security

Security for Active Construction Sites

Construction sites in New York City face persistent security problems. Expensive tools, raw materials, copper wiring, and heavy equipment are all attractive targets for thieves. Trespassing creates liability exposure. And the site itself changes week to week, which means the security plan has to adapt along with it.

Blue Core Shield assigns guards who understand how construction projects operate. They manage gate access, check worker credentials, verify deliveries, and patrol laydown areas and equipment yards. As your project moves through different phases, we adjust staffing and coverage to match the current risks on site.

We work with general contractors, developers, and construction managers on projects of all sizes, from small residential renovations to large commercial builds. Our officers coordinate with site superintendents and are comfortable working around active construction activity.

What We Cover

Typical Coverage Positions

Construction security is built around the current phase of your project and the specific risks at your site.

Main Gate Post

Officers who control vehicle and pedestrian access, check worker badges and union cards, and verify all deliveries entering the site.

Laydown and Equipment Yard

Dedicated patrols of material staging areas, tool cribs, and equipment storage to prevent theft and unauthorized removal of assets.

Perimeter and Fence Line

Walking and mobile patrols along the site perimeter, especially during overnight hours when most break-ins and theft attempts occur.

Interior Building Patrols

Officers who walk through partially completed structures to check for trespassers, fire hazards, and signs of unauthorized activity.
