Residential Security

Security for Residential Properties

Residents expect to feel safe where they live. Whether it is a high-rise condo on the Upper East Side, a co-op in Park Slope, or a gated community on Staten Island, the right security presence makes a real difference in how people experience their home.

Blue Core Shield provides lobby attendants, gatehouse officers, and patrol services for residential properties throughout New York City. Our guards build relationships with the people who live in the buildings they protect. They learn names, recognize routines, and can tell when something is out of place. That kind of familiarity is one of the most effective security tools there is.

We work with property managers, HOA boards, and co-op boards to design security programs that balance safety with the welcoming atmosphere residents want. Our officers handle package management, visitor screening, parking enforcement, and common area monitoring while treating every resident with courtesy and respect.

Common Posts

Typical Coverage Positions

Residential security is all about consistency. These are the posts where our officers make the biggest impact for residents.

Lobby and Front Desk

A welcoming but watchful presence in the building lobby, managing visitor check-in, package handling, and building access throughout the day and night.

Gatehouse and Vehicle Entry

Officers who control vehicle access for gated communities and parking structures, verifying residents and screening guests before entry.

Community Patrols

Walking and mobile patrols through hallways, stairwells, parking areas, and common spaces to maintain a visible and reassuring presence.

Amenity Area Monitoring

Coverage for pools, fitness centers, rooftop terraces, and other shared amenities where rules enforcement and safety oversight are needed.
