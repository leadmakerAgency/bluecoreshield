Warehouse Security

Security for Warehouses and Distribution Centers

Warehouses and distribution facilities are high-value targets. The inventory inside can be worth millions, and the constant movement of goods creates opportunities for internal and external theft. A single unmonitored loading dock or an unverified delivery driver can lead to significant losses.

Blue Core Shield assigns security officers who are trained specifically for logistics environments. They monitor dock activity, verify inbound and outbound shipments, control who enters the facility, and patrol truck courts and perimeter areas throughout the day and night. Our guards pay close attention to shift change windows and overnight hours when most theft occurs.

We work with fulfillment centers, cold storage facilities, last-mile distribution hubs, and general warehousing operations. Our officers understand seal integrity, bill of lading verification, and the signs of organized cargo theft so they can flag problems before they become major losses.

What We Cover

Typical Coverage Positions

Warehouse security coverage is built around the specific layout and operations of your facility.

Gate and Entry Control

Officers stationed at vehicle and pedestrian gates to verify credentials, check delivery paperwork, and log all arrivals and departures.

Loading Dock Monitoring

Dedicated dock officers who watch inbound and outbound activity, verify seal numbers, and ensure only authorized personnel access the loading area.

Interior Warehouse Patrols

Walking patrols through storage aisles, high-value inventory zones, and staging areas to deter internal theft and detect hazards.

Perimeter and Truck Court

Vehicle and foot patrols covering the fence line, truck parking areas, and exterior grounds to prevent unauthorized access.
