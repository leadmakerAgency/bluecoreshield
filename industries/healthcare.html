Healthcare Security

Security for Healthcare Environments

Hospitals, clinics, and medical offices present security challenges that are different from any other industry. Patients and visitors may be stressed, frightened, or in crisis. Staff members need to feel protected so they can focus on providing care. And certain areas like pharmacies, emergency departments, and behavioral health units require a higher level of attention.

Blue Core Shield provides security officers who receive specialized training in de-escalation techniques, patient interaction protocols, and HIPAA awareness. Our guards understand that their presence should make people feel safer, not more anxious. They know how to handle difficult situations calmly and how to work alongside clinical staff without getting in the way of patient care.

We serve hospitals, urgent care centers, outpatient clinics, behavioral health facilities, and private medical practices. Our officers are trained to enforce visitor policies, manage waiting room situations, respond to code calls, and secure restricted areas including pharmacies and records rooms.

What We Cover

Typical Coverage Positions

Healthcare security requires officers who can adapt to fast-changing situations while maintaining a calm and professional presence.

Emergency Department

Officers posted at the ED entrance and waiting area to manage visitor flow, assist with agitated patients, and respond to security codes quickly.

Main Entrance and Lobby

Front-of-house security that screens visitors, enforces after-hours access policies, and directs patients and families to the right departments.

Floor and Unit Patrols

Walking patrols through patient floors, behavioral health units, and administrative areas to maintain security and respond to staff calls for help.

Pharmacy and Records

Monitoring access to pharmacies, medication storage, records rooms, and other restricted areas where sensitive materials or controlled substances are kept.
