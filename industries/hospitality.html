Hospitality Security

Security for Hotels and Hospitality

Hotels operate around the clock and welcome a constant flow of guests, vendors, and staff. Security in this environment has to be effective without being intrusive. Guests need to feel safe, but they should not feel like they are under surveillance. That balance requires officers who are trained in hospitality as much as they are trained in security.

Blue Core Shield provides discreet, professional security for hotels, resorts, and extended-stay properties across New York City. Our officers patrol common areas, monitor parking facilities, manage after-hours access, and respond to incidents with the kind of professionalism that reflects well on your brand. They are trained to handle noise complaints, unauthorized visitors, and guest disputes without creating scenes.

We work with boutique hotels, large chain properties, and independent operators. Whether you need a single overnight officer or a full security team for a busy Midtown hotel, we build the program around your guest experience standards and your operational needs.

Common Posts

Typical Coverage Positions

Hotel security is about being present where it matters without being in the way. These are the posts we staff most often.

Lobby and Front Entrance

Officers who maintain a welcoming presence at the main entrance, assist with guest safety concerns, and screen for unauthorized visitors.

Guest Floor Patrols

Quiet, regular patrols through guest floor hallways, stairwells, and elevator lobbies to deter problems before they start.

Parking and Exterior

Coverage of parking garages, valet areas, and the exterior perimeter of the property to protect guest vehicles and control access.

Event and Banquet Areas

Security staffing for conferences, weddings, and private events held on hotel property, including access control and crowd management.
