Running a secure commercial property in New York requires more than a guard at the front desk.

Use this checklist to review your current setup:

- Confirm that entry points are covered by both policy and personnel.
- Verify access control for visitors, vendors, and after-hours staff.
- Review patrol frequency and incident reporting standards.
- Test emergency communication flow between site staff and supervisors.
- Reassess coverage after tenant or occupancy changes.
