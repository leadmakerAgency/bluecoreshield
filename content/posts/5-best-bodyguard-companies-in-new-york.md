## **Critical Factors in Selecting a Close Protection Team**

Hiring a personal bodyguard or close protection detail requires a much more specific evaluation process than contracting standard building security guards. Entrusting your personal safety or that of an executive team to an outside vendor means looking closely at three vital metrics.

### **Advanced Planning and Threat Assessments**

An elite bodyguard detail spends the majority of its operational energy preventing a security incident from ever occurring. When evaluating firms, ensure they prioritize rigorous "advance" work. This includes conducting physical sweeps of meeting venues, mapping out secondary and tertiary travel emergency escape routes, analyzing local hospital capabilities near your destination, and continuously evaluating active intelligence channels for localized risks.

### **Behavioral Discretion and Tactical De-escalation**

The ideal protective asset should act as an elegant, professional extension of your entourage rather than a disruptive physical obstacle. Prioritize protection agencies that train their personnel to remain calm under immense pressure, dress appropriately for high-end corporate or social environments, and resolve potential conflicts using expert situational awareness and de-escalation techniques before resorting to physical force.

### **Verified Licensing and Specialized Liability Coverage**

Personal protection involving armed escorts demands total alignment with state and local laws. In New York, always confirm that the protection specialists are fully licensed under the New York State Department of State Division of Licensing Services and hold the necessary carry permits for the specific jurisdictions you will traverse. Additionally, verify that the firm maintains comprehensive executive protection insurance policies that cover specialized professional liability and high-limit bodily injury provisions.
