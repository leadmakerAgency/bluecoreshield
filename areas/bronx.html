Security Services in the Bronx

From the industrial hub at Hunts Point to the commercial corridor along the Grand Concourse, Blue Core Shield provides trained security personnel throughout the Bronx.

OVERVIEW

Bronx Security Operations

The Bronx has one of the most diverse property landscapes in New York City. Hunts Point is the backbone of the city's food supply chain, with produce markets, meat distributors, and fish wholesalers operating around the clock. The Grand Concourse and Fordham Road are major commercial corridors with retail, offices, and healthcare facilities. And throughout the borough, large residential complexes and affordable housing developments require consistent security coverage.

Blue Core Shield has been active in the Bronx for years. We provide guards for food distribution facilities in Hunts Point and Port Morris, construction sites along the waterfront and in Mott Haven, healthcare campuses near Montefiore and Lincoln Medical Center, and residential properties from Pelham Bay to Kingsbridge. Our teams understand the operational tempo of each neighborhood and staff accordingly.

The Bronx is also experiencing significant new development. The South Bronx waterfront, the area around Yankee Stadium, and neighborhoods along the Metro-North corridor are all seeing new construction. We provide security for these projects from the ground-breaking phase through tenant move-in.

NEIGHBORHOODS

Where We Operate in the Bronx

Our coverage spans the entire borough. These are the neighborhoods and districts where we maintain the strongest presence.

Hunts Point and Port Morris

The city's primary food distribution hub. Produce markets, cold storage facilities, meat processing operations, and logistics companies that run 24 hours a day.

Mott Haven and the South Bronx

New residential developments, waterfront construction projects, commercial lofts, and mixed-use properties in one of the borough's fastest-changing areas.

Grand Concourse and Fordham

Commercial offices, healthcare facilities, retail along Fordham Road, and residential buildings lining the Grand Concourse from 149th Street to Mosholu Parkway.

Norwood and Bedford Park

Healthcare campuses, university facilities near Lehman College, residential properties, and commercial corridors in the central Bronx.

Pelham Bay and Throgs Neck

Residential communities, retail strips, and commercial properties in the eastern Bronx, including areas near Pelham Bay Park and the Hutchinson River Parkway corridor.

Kingsbridge and Riverdale

Residential buildings, private schools, retail along Broadway and Riverdale Avenue, and properties near the Van Cortlandt Park area in the northwest Bronx.

SERVICES

Available Services in the Bronx

Every service we offer is available throughout the Bronx. These are the programs our Bronx clients request most frequently.

Unarmed Security

Standing posts and lobby officers for residential complexes, healthcare facilities, commercial properties, and retail locations throughout the borough.

Mobile Patrol

Vehicle and foot patrols for industrial zones, large residential developments, and commercial properties that benefit from regular rounds and visible deterrence.

Construction Security

Gate control, material protection, and overnight coverage for the Bronx's growing number of new construction and renovation projects.

Fire Watch

Certified fire watch personnel for any Bronx property with impaired fire protection systems, available on short notice.
