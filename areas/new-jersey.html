Security Services in Northern New Jersey

Blue Core Shield serves clients across the Hudson River in Hudson, Bergen, Essex, and Passaic counties. The same standards, the same training, and the same reliability we deliver in New York.

OVERVIEW

Northern New Jersey Security Operations

Northern New Jersey is a natural extension of our New York City operations. Jersey City and Hoboken have become major commercial and residential centers, with office towers along the waterfront, luxury apartment buildings, and a growing retail and hospitality scene. Newark is home to one of the region's busiest airports, a large healthcare system, and a commercial district that is seeing renewed investment. And throughout Bergen, Passaic, and Essex counties, there are corporate offices, warehouse operations, shopping centers, and residential communities that all require trained security personnel.

Blue Core Shield operates in New Jersey in full compliance with state regulatory requirements for private security agencies. Our guards assigned to New Jersey accounts hold the credentials required under applicable state law. We understand that New Jersey has its own regulatory requirements for private security, and we handle all of that compliance so our clients do not have to worry about it. Our NJ teams coordinate directly with our NYC operations center, which gives us the ability to scale up coverage on short notice.

Many of our New Jersey clients are companies that also have offices or properties in Manhattan, and they chose us because they wanted a single security vendor for both sides of the river. We make that transition seamless, with consistent reporting, the same communication protocols, and uniform standards across every location.

COVERAGE AREAS

Where We Operate in Northern New Jersey

We serve clients across four counties in Northern New Jersey. These are the areas where we maintain the most active operations.

Jersey City and Hoboken

Waterfront office towers, residential high-rises, retail corridors, and the growing commercial district along the Hudson River. One of the most active markets in the region.

Newark

The Ironbound district, downtown corporate offices, healthcare facilities near University Hospital, and commercial properties throughout New Jersey's largest city.

Bergen County

Corporate offices in Paramus and Hackensack, retail centers including Garden State Plaza, medical facilities, and residential communities throughout the county.

Secaucus and the Meadowlands

Warehouse and distribution facilities, outlet shopping centers, hotels, and commercial properties in the logistics corridor near the New Jersey Turnpike.

Passaic County

Commercial properties in Paterson and Clifton, industrial facilities, healthcare campuses, and retail centers along the Route 46 and Route 3 corridors.

Essex County

Corporate offices in Montclair and Livingston, the South Orange and Maplewood commercial corridors, and residential properties throughout the county's diverse communities.
