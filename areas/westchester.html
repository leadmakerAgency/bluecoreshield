Security Services in Westchester County

From downtown White Plains to the communities along the Hudson and Long Island Sound, Blue Core Shield provides professional security throughout Westchester County.

OVERVIEW

Westchester County Security Operations

Westchester County sits just north of the Bronx and is home to a wide range of properties that require professional security services. White Plains is the county's commercial center, with corporate offices, law firms, medical facilities, and the Westchester Mall drawing thousands of workers and visitors daily. Yonkers, the county's largest city, has a mix of residential complexes, retail centers, and waterfront developments. And communities like New Rochelle, Mount Vernon, Tarrytown, and Harrison each have their own security needs tied to local commercial activity and residential growth.

Blue Core Shield brings the same standards we maintain in New York City to every Westchester account. Our guards are trained and certified to the same protocols we use across all of our service areas. We staff Westchester assignments with personnel who know the county and can navigate between locations efficiently, which is especially important for mobile patrol routes and multi-site accounts.

Many of our Westchester clients are organizations that also have locations in the city, and they value having a single security provider that can deliver consistent service across both markets. We handle the coordination so you do not have to manage multiple vendors.

COVERAGE AREAS

Where We Operate in Westchester

We serve communities throughout Westchester County. These are some of the areas where we have the most active security operations.

White Plains

The county seat and commercial hub. Corporate offices, law firms, retail destinations, government buildings, and the Westchester County Center.

Yonkers

Residential complexes, waterfront developments along the Hudson, shopping centers like Ridge Hill, and commercial properties throughout Westchester's largest city.

New Rochelle

Downtown revitalization projects, residential towers, retail corridors, and healthcare facilities in a city experiencing significant new development.

Tarrytown and the Rivertowns

Corporate campuses, hotels, residential communities, and commercial properties along the Hudson River corridor from Hastings to Sleepy Hollow.

Mount Vernon and Pelham

Residential buildings, retail centers, healthcare offices, and commercial properties in the southern Westchester communities bordering the Bronx.

Harrison and Purchase

Corporate headquarters, office parks along the I-287 corridor, private clubs, and commercial properties in eastern Westchester.
