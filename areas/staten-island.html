Security Services in Staten Island

Staten Island's growing commercial districts, residential communities, and industrial properties all need reliable security. Blue Core Shield provides full coverage across the borough.

OVERVIEW

Staten Island Security Operations

Staten Island has a different character from the other boroughs, but the security needs are just as real. The West Shore industrial corridor along the Arthur Kill is home to warehouses, distribution facilities, and manufacturing operations that require overnight patrols and access control. The commercial areas around St. George and the ferry terminal see steady foot traffic and need visible security presence. And the borough's many residential developments, shopping centers, and corporate parks all benefit from professional guard services.

Blue Core Shield serves clients throughout Staten Island. We provide standing posts for retail centers like the Staten Island Mall area, gate officers for construction sites, patrol services for corporate office parks, and lobby security for residential buildings. Our guards assigned to Staten Island know the borough's layout and can respond quickly when situations change.

As new development continues along the North Shore and the West Shore, we are expanding our presence to meet growing demand. If you have a property in Staten Island that needs security, we can get officers on site quickly and build a long-term program around your needs.

NEIGHBORHOODS

Where We Operate in Staten Island

We provide security services across the entire borough. These are the key areas where we maintain active operations.

St. George and the North Shore

The ferry terminal area, government buildings, new waterfront developments, and commercial properties along Bay Street and Richmond Terrace.

West Shore Industrial Corridor

Warehouses, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities along the Arthur Kill waterfront, including properties near the Goethals Bridge crossing.

New Dorp and Midland Beach

Retail corridors along New Dorp Lane and Hylan Boulevard, commercial plazas, medical offices, and surrounding residential neighborhoods.

Tottenville and Great Kills

Residential communities, local commercial centers, and properties along the southern shore of the island.

Stapleton and Tompkinsville

Mixed-use developments, new residential construction, and commercial revitalization projects in the neighborhoods adjacent to the ferry corridor.

Travis and Bulls Head

Shopping centers, corporate office parks, and residential properties in central Staten Island near the Staten Island Expressway corridor.
