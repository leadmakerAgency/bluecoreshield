Security Services on Long Island

Blue Core Shield extends our security coverage across Nassau and Suffolk counties. Corporate campuses, healthcare facilities, retail centers, and residential communities throughout Long Island.

OVERVIEW

Long Island Security Operations

Long Island presents a different security environment from the five boroughs, but the need for trained, professional guards is just as strong. Nassau County is home to major corporate offices in Garden City and Lake Success, shopping destinations like Roosevelt Field Mall and the Americana Manhasset, and dense residential communities throughout Hempstead, Freeport, and the surrounding towns. Suffolk County has a growing base of corporate campuses in Melville and Hauppauge, healthcare facilities, industrial parks, and thousands of commercial properties that need regular security coverage.

Blue Core Shield serves Long Island clients with the same level of professionalism and reliability we bring to our NYC operations. We staff guards who are familiar with the suburban layout of the island, the traffic patterns, and the property types common in each area. Whether you manage a medical office park in Great Neck, a warehouse in Farmingdale, or a gated residential community in the Hamptons, we have the personnel and the experience to handle it.

Our Long Island teams are supported by our NYC operations center, which means you get the resources of a large security company with the responsiveness of a local provider. We can deploy additional guards on short notice and maintain consistent coverage even during staffing transitions.

COVERAGE AREAS

Where We Operate on Long Island

We serve clients across both Nassau and Suffolk counties. These are some of the key areas where we have active security operations.

Garden City and Mineola

Corporate offices, law firms, medical practices, and commercial properties in Nassau County's primary business district, centered around Old Country Road and Franklin Avenue.

Hempstead and Roosevelt Field

One of Long Island's busiest retail and commercial corridors, including Roosevelt Field Mall, surrounding shopping centers, and the commercial offices along the Meadowbrook Parkway area.

Melville and Hauppauge

Suffolk County's corporate corridor along the Long Island Expressway, home to major office parks, technology companies, and business campuses.

Great Neck and Lake Success

Medical office complexes, corporate headquarters, and upscale commercial properties in the North Shore communities of western Nassau County.

Farmingdale and Bethpage

Industrial parks, manufacturing facilities, distribution centers, and commercial properties along the Route 110 corridor in central Long Island.

South Shore Communities

Residential developments, retail centers, and commercial properties in communities along the South Shore, from Long Beach to Babylon and beyond.

SERVICES

Available Services on Long Island

Our full range of security services is available to Long Island clients. Here are the programs most commonly requested in Nassau and Suffolk counties.

Unarmed Security

Lobby officers, access control, and standing posts for corporate campuses, medical facilities, retail centers, and residential communities.

Mobile Patrol

Vehicle patrols for office parks, industrial areas, shopping centers, and large properties where regular rounds provide effective coverage.

Armed Security

Licensed armed officers for financial institutions, jewelry stores, high-value commercial properties, and any location requiring a heightened security presence.

Event Security

Trained event personnel for corporate functions, private events, galas, and outdoor gatherings at Long Island venues and estates.
