Security Services in Brooklyn

Brooklyn is one of our most active service areas. From the waterfront developments in Williamsburg to the industrial corridors of Sunset Park, we have guards working across the borough every day.

OVERVIEW

Brooklyn Security Operations

Brooklyn has changed rapidly over the past two decades, and the security needs have changed with it. New residential towers in Downtown Brooklyn and along the Williamsburg waterfront require lobby officers and concierge security. The warehouse and manufacturing districts in Sunset Park, East New York, and the Brooklyn Navy Yard need overnight patrols and access control. Retail corridors along Atlantic Avenue, Fulton Street, and Fifth Avenue in Park Slope call for loss prevention and storefront coverage.

Blue Core Shield has been providing security services in Brooklyn since our earliest days. We understand the layout of the borough, the traffic patterns, and the differences between neighborhoods. A security plan that works for a Dumbo office building is not the same as what you need for a Red Hook warehouse. We build programs that fit the specific property and the specific neighborhood.

Our Brooklyn teams include guards who live in the borough and know the streets they patrol. That local knowledge makes a measurable difference in how quickly problems get identified and resolved.

NEIGHBORHOODS

Where We Operate in Brooklyn

We serve every neighborhood in Brooklyn. Here are some of the areas with the highest concentration of our active security accounts.

Downtown Brooklyn and DUMBO

Office towers, residential high-rises, tech company headquarters, and retail along Fulton Mall and the surrounding streets. One of Brooklyn's densest commercial districts.

Williamsburg and Greenpoint

Waterfront residential developments, boutique hotels, retail corridors, and mixed-use buildings in two of Brooklyn's most active neighborhoods.

Sunset Park and Industry City

Warehouses, manufacturing facilities, logistics operations, and the Industry City commercial complex along the Third Avenue industrial corridor.

Park Slope and Prospect Heights

Residential brownstones, retail along Fifth and Seventh Avenues, and properties near the Barclays Center and Atlantic Terminal.

Red Hook and Gowanus

Waterfront warehouses, creative studios, and new residential developments in neighborhoods undergoing significant growth.

East New York and Canarsie

Industrial properties, distribution centers, residential complexes, and retail locations in Brooklyn's eastern neighborhoods.

SERVICES

Available Services in Brooklyn

All of our security services are available throughout Brooklyn. These are the programs we deploy most often in the borough.

Lobby officers, concierge teams, and standing posts for residential buildings, office properties, and retail locations throughout the borough.

Construction Security
