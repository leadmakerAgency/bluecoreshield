Security Services in Queens

Queens is home to some of the city's largest warehouse districts, busiest commercial corridors, and most diverse residential communities. We provide security coverage across the entire borough.

OVERVIEW

Queens Security Operations

Queens is the largest borough by area, and the security landscape reflects that size. Long Island City has become a major hub for office space, residential towers, and creative studios. Maspeth and Ridgewood host warehouse and logistics operations that need overnight patrols and access control. Flushing is one of the city's busiest commercial districts, with retail, restaurants, and office space generating constant foot traffic. And neighborhoods like Jamaica, Forest Hills, and Bayside have growing residential and commercial properties that require reliable security coverage.

Blue Core Shield has active accounts throughout Queens, from the waterfront in Astoria to the residential blocks of Howard Beach. Our guards work in the industrial parks along the Long Island Expressway, the retail centers near Queens Center Mall, and the healthcare facilities clustered around Jamaica and Flushing. We understand the geographic spread of the borough and staff accordingly.

Queens also includes JFK Airport's surrounding commercial zone, with hotels, logistics companies, and cargo operations that all require security personnel. We serve clients in that corridor and throughout the borough's many distinct neighborhoods.

NEIGHBORHOODS

Where We Operate in Queens

We cover every part of Queens. These are the neighborhoods and districts where we maintain the most active operations.

Long Island City

Office towers, residential high-rises, creative studios, and warehouse conversions in Queens' fastest-growing neighborhood along the East River waterfront.

Astoria and Woodside

Mixed-use commercial strips, residential buildings, restaurants, and retail along Steinway Street, Broadway, and the surrounding blocks.

Maspeth and Ridgewood

Industrial warehouses, distribution centers, manufacturing facilities, and logistics operations in one of the city's key supply chain corridors.

Flushing

One of Queens' busiest commercial hubs with retail, restaurants, offices, and residential towers concentrated around Main Street and Roosevelt Avenue.

Jamaica

Commercial offices, healthcare facilities, transit hubs, and residential developments in central Queens. A growing area with increasing security demand.

JFK Corridor and Howard Beach

Hotels, cargo facilities, logistics companies, and commercial properties in the area surrounding John F. Kennedy International Airport.
