Where We Operate

We cover all five boroughs of New York City and extend into the surrounding tri-state region. Wherever your property is, we can get guards on site.

New York City

All Five Boroughs Covered

Our core operations are based in New York City. We have guards stationed throughout every borough and can deploy to any location on short notice.

Manhattan

From the Financial District to Washington Heights, we protect corporate towers, retail locations, residential buildings, and construction sites across the entire island.

Brooklyn

Warehouses in Sunset Park, retail in Downtown Brooklyn, construction in Williamsburg, and residential complexes borough-wide.

Queens

Warehouse districts in Long Island City and Maspeth, commercial properties along Queens Boulevard, and communities from Astoria to Jamaica.

The Bronx

Commercial properties along the Grand Concourse, industrial sites in Hunts Point, healthcare facilities, and residential complexes throughout the borough.

Staten Island

Shopping centers, corporate parks, gated communities, and construction sites across the borough's growing commercial and residential developments.

Long Island

Nassau and Suffolk counties. Corporate campuses, retail centers, industrial parks, and residential communities across both counties.
Extended Region

Tri-State Area Coverage

Beyond the five boroughs, we serve clients in the surrounding counties and neighboring states.

Westchester County

From White Plains to Yonkers, we provide security for commercial properties, hospitals, schools, and residential developments throughout Westchester.

Northern New Jersey

Hudson, Bergen, Essex, and Passaic counties. Warehouse and logistics operations, corporate offices, retail locations, and multi-family residential properties.

Local Advantage

Why a Local Security Partner Matters

New York is not one city. It is dozens of neighborhoods, each with its own dynamics. We staff guards who know the area they are assigned to.

Faster Response Times

Our teams are positioned throughout the city. When you need additional coverage, we can get people to your location quickly because we already have guards nearby.

Neighborhood Familiarity

Guards who know their patrol area spot problems faster. They recognize what is normal and what is not. That awareness comes from working the same area consistently.

Community Relationships

Our officers build working relationships with local businesses, residents, and law enforcement. Those connections make the entire security operation more effective.

Local Compliance Knowledge

New York has specific licensing, insurance, and regulatory requirements. We handle all of that so you do not have to worry about whether your vendor is properly credentialed.
